    PSOBB Texture Editor
    Three steps to a deployed texture upgrade.
    1
    Pick a file
    Click any .prs or .xvm on the left. PRS files decompress automatically; XVM files load directly. Both end up as a grid of tiles.
    2
    Upscale
    Press U to upscale every tile, or click a single tile to enter its A/B view. Run external tools (e.g. Upscayl) and drag the result onto a tile card to import.
    3
    Repack & deploy
    Press R. The editor shows a diff of what's about to change, makes a timestamped backup, repacks the original container, and copies it back to the live data/.
    tip: Ctrl+F filters files, / filters tiles inside a file, click+drag a tile card to A/B compare in place.
